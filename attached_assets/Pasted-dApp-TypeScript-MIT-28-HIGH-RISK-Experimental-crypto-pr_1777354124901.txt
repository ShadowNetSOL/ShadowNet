CODE OVERVIEW
This TypeScript repository appears to be a Solana-focused privacy dApp that combines browser-based wallet generation, relay-based traffic routing, and session privacy features. It also mentions wallet analytics and contract-address intelligence tooling. In plain terms, it aims to let users browse or interact with Solana apps with reduced tracking while creating disposable wallets locally in the browser.

SECURITY RISKS
Browser-generated wallet flows can expose mnemonics if frontend code, hosting, or dependencies are compromised
Relay operators may log IPs, metadata, destinations, or timing data despite privacy claims
Experimental privacy tooling may give users a false sense of anonymity
If users import generated keys into wallets, compromise could lead to direct fund theft
Low adoption and young age mean fewer eyes reviewing code for backdoors or mistakes
Public fallback relay services increase third-party trust and surveillance exposure
